Splash Quiz

Quiz rules

  • This is a multiple choice question (MCQ) type quiz.
  • You need to attempt all the five questions.
  • Need to select one correct option from 4 available options.
  • You will have only 30 seconds for each question to answer.
  • Timer will be visible on your screen.
  • Once you select the answer you can't reselect.
  • Option can't be selected once you are out of time.
  • You get 10 points for each correct answer.
